## 2. Surcharges & Optional Upgrades

| Surcharge | Amount | When It Applies |
|---|---|---|
| Prime Service | +$5 + tax | OPTIONAL: Door-to-door delivery + free second delivery attempt. No change in delivery speed. All service areas. |
| Medium Parcel | +$4 + tax | TOTAL combined volume > Standard, fits within 60×31×31 cm, ≤ 30 lb. |
| Large Parcel | +$9 + tax | TOTAL combined volume > Medium, fits within 80×38×38 cm, ≤ 50 lb. |
| Zone A Express | +$5 | OPTIONAL: Afternoon Express delivery (13:30-17:00) for Etobicoke, York, North York West, Toronto Downtown West. |
| Zone B Express | +$8 | OPTIONAL: Afternoon Express delivery (13:30-17:00) for Mississauga, Vaughan, Oakville. |

**NOTE**: The Flat Rate Afternoon Delivery zone already includes afternoon delivery (13:30-17:00) at the standard base price. Express surcharges only apply to Zone A and Zone B postal codes that are NOT in the Flat Rate Afternoon zone.

## 3. Total Price Formula

`Total Price = Region Base Price + Prime (optional) + Size Surcharge (if applicable) + Express Upgrade (optional, Zone A/B only) + tax`

- Example 1: Individual sending to M4W (Flat Rate Afternoon zone): $12.99 + tax. Afternoon delivery 13:30-17:00 included at no extra charge.
- Example 2: Business (4-9 pkg) sending Medium order to Hamilton (L8P): $9.99 + $4.00 = $13.99 + tax.
- Example 3: Individual sending to M9A (Zone A, not in Flat Rate Afternoon): $12.99 + $5 Express (optional) + tax.
